Leads with the May 2026 ruling as the credibility hook. Positions HaulPay's double-sided transaction verification as the solution. High urgency, executive-level relevance.

  • Step 2 (Day 3): HaulPay's fraud verification solution — 1 specific data point

Track B Operational Pain / Back-Office Automation
"How many hours a week does your team spend chasing invoices? Most brokers we talk to say 10+."
Opens with operational pain, not product pitch. Targets back-office decision-makers. Emphasizes automation, time savings, and error reduction. Avoids factoring language entirely.

  • Step 2 (Day 4): Specific workflow HaulPay automates (invoicing in seconds)
  • Step 3 (Day 9): Social proof / carrier satisfaction angle
  • Step 4 (Day 16): Short video or checklist CTA
Track C Speed & Cash Flow / Same-Day Pay
"What if your carriers got paid today — and you eliminated payment risk entirely at the same time?"
Cash flow and payment speed angle for brokers who manage high carrier volume. Leads with the fastest pay benefit. Free same-day ACH is the differentiator vs. traditional factors.

  • Step 2 (Day 4): Same-day ACH + zero reserve hold-back differentiator
