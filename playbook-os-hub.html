Governance Document

The Rules the Agent Lives By

The Agent Constitution defines the operating boundary of the entire system. No agent may act outside it.

What the Agent Can Optimize

  • ICP fit and contact scoring
  • List segmentation and targeting
  • Message personalization (within approved fields)
  • Subject line variants and A/B testing
  • Pain angle framing (within approved set)
  • CTA style and phrasing
  • Follow-up timing and sequence spacing
  • Sequence branching logic
  • Lead scoring thresholds
  • CRM routing rules
  • Reporting insights and performance summaries

What the Agent Cannot Change

  • Product truth, pricing, or terms
  • Legal or compliance claims
  • Customer references or case study results
  • Guarantees or SLAs of any kind
  • Security or data handling claims
  • Brand voice outside approved range
  • Sending volume limits or domain pool
  • Suppression rules or DNC handling
  • Unsubscribe processing
  • The approved offer structure

When the Agent Must Stop

  • Spam complaint rate rises above threshold
  • Bounce rate exceeds domain safety limit
  • Negative reply rate spikes materially
  • Domain health score drops
  • Message claims cannot be confirmed accurate
  • A prospect has asked to stop receiving mail
  • A reply requires legal, pricing, security, or executive judgment
  • Deliverability Agent issues a veto

This constitution governs the entire system. Any modification to these rules requires written approval from both Shaun O'Farrell (Playbook Ventures LLC) and Steve Kochan (HaulPay / Dakota Financial LLC).

Authority Structure

What HaulPay Always Controls

The system operates under a defined authority structure. These rights belong to HaulPay — exclusively and irrevocably.

📄
All Campaign Copy
Nothing sends without written approval from HaulPay. Every sequence, every variant, every subject line — reviewed and signed off before it reaches a prospect.
🏷
All Product Claims
The agent never represents terms, pricing, guarantees, or product capabilities without an explicitly approved claim on file. No improvisation on facts.
🌐
Domain & Mailbox Activation
Every domain and mailbox is proposed in the dashboard and requires HaulPay approval before purchase or activation. No infrastructure moves without sign-off.
⏸
Instant Campaign Pause
One click in the dashboard pauses all active sends across every sequence, domain, and mailbox. No escalation needed. Immediate.
⚖️

Any change to the rules governing agent behavior requires written sign-off from both Steve Kochan (HaulPay) and Shaun O'Farrell (Playbook Ventures). The rules cannot change without HaulPay's consent.
